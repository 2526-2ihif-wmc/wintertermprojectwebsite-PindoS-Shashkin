What is War Thunder?

War Thunder is a free-to-play online military vehicle combat game developed by Gaijin Entertainment and released on December 21st, 2016. It focuses on realistic battles using vehicles from the 1930s up to modern day.

My Experience

This website is made to help you understand what War Thunder is and to share my personal experience with the game. I enjoy playing tanks, aircraft, and naval battles.
