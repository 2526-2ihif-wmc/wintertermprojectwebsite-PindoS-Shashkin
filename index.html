What is War Thunder?

This website is made to help you understand what War Thunder is. War Thunder is a free-to-play online military vehicle combat game developed by Gaijin Entertainment and released on December 21st, 2016. It focuses on realistic battles using vehicles from the 1930s up to modern day. It has more then 2800 vehicles.
