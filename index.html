What is War Thunder?

This website is made to help you understand what War Thunder is. War Thunder is a free-to-play online military vehicle combat game developed by Gaijin Entertainment and released on December 21st, 2016. It focuses on realistic battles using vehicles from the 1930s up to modern day. It has more then 2800 vehicles.

My Experience

To start with, I’d like to share my personal experience with the game. I enjoy playing across all modes—tanks, aircraft, and naval battles—which makes the variety especially appealing. As mentioned earlier, War Thunder is a free-to-play game, and that naturally raises the ongoing controversy: is it truly free-to-play, or does it lean toward pay-to-win? In many ways, the debate is understandable. A free-to-play model still needs to generate revenue, and the question becomes how fairly that balance is struck between paying players and those who prefer to play without spending money.
