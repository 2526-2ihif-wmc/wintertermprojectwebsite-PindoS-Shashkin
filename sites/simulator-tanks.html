Overview

Simulator Tank Battles represent the highest level of realism and difficulty in War Thunder. Interface elements are almost entirely removed, vehicle handling is uncompromising, and success depends purely on player skill, situational awareness, and teamwork. This mode is designed for experienced players seeking maximum immersion.

Key Features of Simulator Mode

  • No HUD elements, markers, or aim assistance.
  • Restricted camera views – gunner and commander perspectives matter.
  • Friendly fire enabled, including aircraft.
  • Very limited respawns.
  • Strong reliance on sound, map knowledge, and communication.

Tank Classes – Simulator Mode Impact

Tank classes in Simulator Battles are identical to other modes, but their effectiveness is heavily influenced by visibility, crew positions, and realistic engagement distances. Mistakes are costly, and survival often matters more than aggression.
