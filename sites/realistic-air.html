Overview

Realistic Air Battles in War Thunder provide a more authentic flying experience compared to Arcade mode. Flight physics are closer to reality, aiming assistance is reduced, and situational awareness becomes crucial. Players must rely on energy management, positioning, and teamwork to succeed.

Key Features

  • More realistic flight physics and aerodynamics.
  • No visible enemy markers – spotting is manual.
  • Limited respawns depending on aircraft lineup.
  • Greater emphasis on tactics, energy fighting, and teamwork.
