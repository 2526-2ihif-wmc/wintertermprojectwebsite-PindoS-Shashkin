Overview

Arcade Air Battles are the most accessible and fast-paced air combat mode in War Thunder. Aircraft performance is boosted, controls are simplified, and players are encouraged to stay constantly engaged in combat. This mode focuses on fun, speed, and action rather than realism.

Key Features of Arcade Air Battles

  • Simplified flight physics with increased maneuverability.
  • Lead indicator for gunnery.
  • Multiple respawns per match.
  • Shorter match duration and constant action.

Aircraft Classes – Arcade Mode Impact

Aircraft classes in Arcade Battles behave differently compared to other modes. Boosted engine power, simplified stall behavior, and quick re-engagement significantly reduce the skill gap between aircraft types. The emphasis is placed on reaction speed and aggressive play.
