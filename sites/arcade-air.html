Overview

Arcade Air Battles in War Thunder are designed to be fast, dynamic, and accessible. Aircraft are easier to control, aiming assistance is available, and respawns allow for continuous action. This mode is perfect for beginners or players who enjoy quick dogfights without strict realism.

Key Features

  • Simplified flight physics for easier maneuvering.
  • Aiming assistance for gunnery.
  • Multiple respawns per match.
  • Fast-paced aerial combat with shorter battle times.
