Overview

Naval battles in War Thunder are often considered slow and dull. Ships take a long time to maneuver, engagements happen at great distances, and the pace of combat lacks the intensity found in tank or air battles. Because of this, the naval mode attracts fewer players compared to other parts of the game.

Why Naval Battles Feel Unpopular

The large maps and long travel times make battles feel drawn out. Maneuvering ships requires patience, and the slow pace of engagements can feel monotonous.
