Overview

Simulator Air Battles in War Thunder are the most immersive and demanding mode. There are no HUD markers, flight physics are fully realistic, and situational awareness depends entirely on the pilot. Success requires patience, precision, and mastery of aircraft mechanics.

Key Features

  • Full realism: authentic flight physics, aerodynamics, and ballistics.
  • No HUD assistance – players must rely on vision and sound.
  • Strict limitations on respawns.
  • High emphasis on teamwork, communication, and tactical positioning.
