Overview

Arcade Tank Battles are designed to be fast-paced and accessible. Enemy markers, simplified physics, and aiming assistance allow players to focus on action rather than advanced vehicle knowledge. This mode is especially suitable for new players or those who prefer quick and dynamic matches.

Key Features of Arcade Mode

  • Visible enemy markers and penetration indicators.
  • Simplified physics and enhanced vehicle performance.
  • Multiple respawns during a single match.
  • Faster gameplay compared to Realistic and Simulator Battles.
  • Aircraft and helicopter spawns as rewards for effective gameplay.

Tank Classes – Arcade-Specific Behavior

Tank classes in War Thunder remain the same across all battle modes. However, Arcade Battles significantly alter how these classes perform. Increased visibility, simplified mechanics, and faster pacing strongly affect vehicle roles and overall effectiveness.

Tank Class Arcade Gameplay Impact Notes
Light Tanks High survivability due to speed and quick repairs More aggressive flanking, scouting less important
Medium Tanks Highly versatile and forgiving Ideal for new players
Heavy Tanks Armor effectiveness reduced by penetration indicators Positioning matters more than angling
Tank Destroyers Long-range advantage reduced by enemy markers Ambush tactics are less effective
SPAA Increased effectiveness due to aircraft markers Strong counter to close air support
