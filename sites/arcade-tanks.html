Overview

Arcade Tank Battles in War Thunder are designed to be fast-paced, accessible, and fun. Physics are simplified, aiming assistance is available, and respawns are allowed. This makes Arcade mode perfect for new players or those who enjoy quick, action-packed matches.

Key Features

  • Simplified tank physics for easier control.
  • Visible enemy markers for quicker engagements.
  • Multiple respawns per battle.
  • Faster gameplay compared to Realistic and Simulator modes.
