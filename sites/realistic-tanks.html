Overview

Realistic Tank Battles in War Thunder provide a more authentic experience compared to Arcade mode. Physics are same as in reality, aiming assistance is reduced, and situational awareness becomes crucial. Players must rely on tactics, positioning, and teamwork to succeed, the most popular game mode in game.

Key Features

  • Realistic tank physics and ballistics.
  • No visible enemy markers – spotting is manual.
  • Limited respawns depending on vehicle lineup.
  • Greater emphasis on tactics and teamwork.
